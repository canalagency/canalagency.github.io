MIKOS BARBERSHOP

Brief:

We approached MIKOS BARBERSHOP to create a vision of the future and show what digital transformation could mean for their customers and their business.

best website templates

The plan:

With a growing number of barbershops arriving in the Canberra area, it was apparent Mikos had to improve their digital marketing to stay on top of the market. We developed personas in order to understand customers needs and motivations, using them to create customer journeys through the business. In tandem, we defined a strategic vision to provide ideas and initiatives in response to what we'd learned about Mikos customers.

Michael Daskalakis

THE WORK

We showed MIKOS BARBERS how they could create a connected customer experience by responding to them in a timely, human way at every key life stage. We gave them instructions on how to run and manage their social media in order to give customers tools that create reasons to engage outside their normal behaviour..
